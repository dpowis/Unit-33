Digging a hole

Always seek the permission of a shovel. Also when ever digging consult you local authority about the underground utilities layout.
Be careful of electrical wires, gas lines and water pipes.
Remember safety first

Get the right supplies for the job. There are many types of holes you can dig and many types of tools you can use.
For virtually all kinds of holes the best tool to use is a shovel.
The shovel is effective, however can be time consuming so you may want to consider automation
Safe time and hire a small digger, as you will get the job done very fast

Finally once you complete the digging of the hole, do not dispose of the soil.
Instead consider the environment and local wildlife. Use the soil to make a nice house for the local badger community
